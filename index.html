Privacy & Trust

HOA documents can contain sensitive property, financial, and community information. HOA Hero is being built for quick, privacy-minded document review.

No accounts

No ads or tracking

Temporary document review

Moderate HOA Risk

Nothing here is an automatic dealbreaker, but you should review reserves, insurance, rental restrictions, and recent board minutes before moving forward.

Watch Closely

  • Low reserves or no reserve study
  • Upcoming special assessments
  • Large insurance increases
  • Rental restrictions or permit requirements

Ask Next

  • Can I see the latest reserve study?
  • Are any assessments planned or being discussed?
  • Have insurance premiums increased recently?
  • Are there rental caps, permits, or waiting periods?
